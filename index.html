Technical Strategy Specifications

1. The Ratchet Logic

The "Ratchet" strategies lock in leverage during rebounds. If a crash hits -20% (Tier 3), it stays at Tier 3 leverage even if the market bounces. It only resets to Tier 0 once a new All-Time High is reached.

2. Asset Matrix (Tier 4 Weights)

Tier 4 (> -30% Drawdown): 50% SSO (2x) / 50% SPYU (4x) = 3.0x Effective Leverage.

3. Calculation Methodology

  • Leveraged Return: (Leverage * SPX) - ((Leverage - 1) * RiskFreeRate)
  • Volatility: Annualized daily standard deviation (Daily Std * sqrt(252)).
  • Sharpe: (CAGR - 2%) / Annualized Volatility.
